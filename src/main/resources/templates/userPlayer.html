Seth Abner (born June 30, 1995), also known as scump , is an American professional Call of Duty player for the Call of Duty League team OpTic Chicago, owned by Hector Rodriguez. Abner is a two time Major League Gaming (MLG) X Games gold medalist. In August 2017, Scump achieved his first ever Call of Duty World Championship.
